Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2025-12-29

This Privacy Policy explains how Skedra ("we", "our", "us") collects, uses, and shares information when you use the Skedra app (the "Service").

Information We Collect

  • Account information: email, display name, profile photo (if you sign in).
  • User content: features you use may store data such as schedules, todos, messages, and social relationships.
  • Diagnostics: crash reports and performance data (e.g., via Firebase Crashlytics).
  • Usage analytics: basic usage events to understand how the app is used (e.g., via Firebase Analytics).
  • Push notification tokens: device/FCM tokens for sending notifications.

How We Use Information

  • Provide and improve the Service
  • Enable social and messaging features (if available)
  • Send notifications you opt into
  • Detect and fix bugs and crashes

How We Share Information

We may share information with service providers that process data on our behalf, such as Firebase (Google) for authentication, database, analytics, crash reporting, and messaging. We do not sell your personal information.

Data Storage

Some data may be stored locally on your device. If you enable sync features, data may be stored and synced using cloud services (e.g., Firebase and/or iCloud, depending on what you enable).

Your Choices

  • You can disable notifications in iOS Settings.
  • You can request account/data deletion via the Support page.
