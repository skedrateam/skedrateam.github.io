Account & Data Deletion

As per Apple's requirements, you can delete your account and all associated data in two ways:

  1. In-App (Recommended): Go to Profile Settings -> Account -> Delete Account. This action is immediate and irreversible.
  2. Email Request: Send an email from your registered address to muhammedkilinc@icloud.com with the subject "Skedra — Account Deletion Request". We will process your request within 48 hours.
