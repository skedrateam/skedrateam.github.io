Account & Data Deletion

If you want to delete your account or request deletion of your data, email us from the email address linked to your Skedra account with:

  • Subject: Skedra — Delete account
  • Your user ID (if available) or the email you used to sign in

Notifications

If you’re not receiving notifications, check iOS Settings → Notifications → Skedra and ensure notifications are enabled.
